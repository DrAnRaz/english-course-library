🏠 Welcome to my flat — by Omar, 10

Hi! I am Omar. I live in a flat with my mum, my dad and my little sister Lina. Our flat is small, but I love it. Lina is six, and she is very funny.

🛋️ The living room

The living room is my favourite room. There is a big blue sofa and a small table. Our cat, Biscuit, sleeps on the sofa every day. There is a TV on the wall, and there are photos of our family on the table.

🛌 My bedroom

My bedroom is next to the bathroom. There is a bed, a desk and a lamp in my room. My football is under the bed. My robot is in the wardrobe — it is my secret!

My books are on the desk. Can you see my school bag? It is on the chair!

🍳 The kitchen

The kitchen is small but happy. Dad cooks dinner there. Right now, there is a pizza in the oven. Yum! The plates are on the table, and the juice is in the fridge. Lina's cup is under her chair — again! Come and visit us!

🔧 Grammar — Where Is It? in / on / under 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, the student says the answer BEFORE you click it.

📦 IN = inside

Something is inside a thing or a room.

The pizza is in the oven. 🍕
My clothes are in the wardrobe.
Dad is in the kitchen.

🔝 ON = on top · ⬇️ UNDER = below

on = touching the top.
The lamp is on the table. 💡

under = below it, hiding!
The football is under the bed. ⚽
💡 The magic question: "Where is the …?" → "It's in / on / under the …"
"Where is the cat?" — "It's on the sofa!" 🐱

💡 Hungarian tip: Hungarian puts the place at the END of the word (szekrényben, asztalon, ágy alatt). English puts a small word BEFORE it: in the wardrobe, on the table, under the bed.
