How to play:

1. Your teacher says an activity, like: "eat pizza for breakfast". 🍕

📅 My week — by Nina, 10

Hi! I'm Nina. Here is my typical week. Can you find all the "how often" words? My week is busy, but I love it.

🌅 My mornings

I always get up at seven o'clock. I usually eat cereal for breakfast, but on Sundays my dad makes pancakes. I am never late for school — well, almost never! My school is ten minutes from my house, and I walk there with my friend Maya.

⚽ After school

On Mondays and Wednesdays I have football practice. I often score a goal, but I rarely score two! On Fridays I sometimes visit my grandma. She always gives me cookies. Grandma's cookies are the best in the world!

🌙 My evenings

I usually do my homework before dinner. After dinner, we often play cards. My little brother never wins, but he always wants to play again! He is five, and he loves the card with the dog on it.

🎉 The weekend

At the weekend I never get up early. On Saturdays I usually ride my bike in the park. Sunday is my favourite day because we always have a family film night. We eat popcorn, and my dad picks funny films.

🔧 Grammar — Where Does the Word Go? 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, student says the answer out loud BEFORE you click it.

✅ BEFORE a normal verb

The frequency word goes before the action.

I always eat breakfast. 🥣
She never watches TV in the morning.
We often play cards.

🔑 Pattern: person + how often + action

⏰ AFTER am / is / are

With the verb be, it goes after.

I am never late. ⏰
She is always happy on Fridays.
They are usually tired after sport.

🔑 Pattern: person + am/is/are + how often
💡 The ladder: always 💯 → usually (90%) → often (70%) → sometimes (50%) → rarely (10%) → never 0️⃣

💡 Hungarian tip: in Hungarian the word order is free — "mindig felkelek / felkelek mindig" both work. In English the place is FIXED. Ask yourself: "Is the verb be? Then AFTER. Any other verb? Then BEFORE."
