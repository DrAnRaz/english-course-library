⚡ Warm-Up — Weather Mime 5 minStep 2
📹 Zoom: cameras on, pure acting game — use the 👍 reaction when the guess is right.

How to play:

1. Your teacher ACTS a weather: brrr, shivering! ☃️ You guess: "It's cold!" "It's snowy!"
2. Now YOU act a weather — no words! Teacher guesses.
3. Extra level: act putting on clothes for that weather. Guess: "A coat!" 🧥

No score here — just wake up your English brain! 🧠

Teacher note — Step 2

Good mimes: hot (fanning), rainy (umbrella hands), windy (blown sideways — be dramatic), sunny (sunglasses). Feed the full sentence back each time: "Yes! IT'S rainy!" Two rounds each is enough.

🌦️ Four seasons, four outfits — by Ravi, 11

Hello! I am Ravi. I live in Manchester, and the weather here changes a lot. Look at my four photos!

❄️ Winter

In this photo, it is snowy and very cold. I'm wearing a warm coat, a red scarf and boots. My gloves are blue.

🌷 Spring

In spring it is often rainy. In this photo, I'm wearing a yellow raincoat and green boots. I love jumping in puddles!

☀️ Summer

It is sunny and hot! I'm wearing a T-shirt, shorts and sunglasses. My dog is wearing a funny hat. He is not happy.

🍂 Autumn

In autumn it is windy and cloudy. I'm wearing a jumper and jeans. Look — the leaves are dancing in the wind!

Teacher note — Step 4 + answer key

Quiz answers: snowy and very cold · blue · his dog · windy and cloudy. Open Q1 key: any true "It's + weather" + "I'm wearing + clothes" sentence — this is the core pattern, insist on "It's" and "I'm". Open Q2: any photo + a simple "because" (because I love snow / dogs are funny). The text repeats "I'm wearing" four times on purpose — point at it before Step 5.
🔧 Grammar — I'm wearing… & It's sunny! 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, the student says the answer BEFORE you click it.

👕 I'M WEARING — clothes on me NOW

I am (I'm) wearing + clothes

I'm wearing a T-shirt.
She's wearing a green dress.
They're wearing hats.

🔑 Never forget am / is / are!

🌤️ IT'S + WEATHER

It's + weather word

It's sunny. ☀️ It's rainy. 🌧️
It's hot. 🥵 It's cold. 🥶

🔑 Weather sentences always start with It's.
🍂 The four seasons: spring 🌷 → summer ☀️ → autumn 🍂 → winter ❄️
"In winter it's snowy, so I'm wearing a coat." — weather + clothes in one super-sentence!

💡 Hungarian tip: in Hungarian you say "Esik" or "Süt a nap" — no "it". English ALWAYS needs the little It's: It's raining, It's sunny. Don't drop it!

Teacher note — Step 5

The two classic errors: dropping "am" (I wearing) and dropping "It's" (Is sunny). Every wrong answer: student repeats the FULL corrected sentence aloud. If 7+/9, bank time for the weather report in Step 6.
