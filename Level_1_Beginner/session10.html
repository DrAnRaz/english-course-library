📖 Reading — The Quest of the Nine Lands 10 minStep 4

Read the quest story. Tap 🔊 to hear any part read aloud.

🗺️ The explorer wakes up

Ben the explorer wakes up at six o'clock. He is brave and friendly, and today is a big day. There is an old map on his desk.

🌲 The journey

He always travels with his dog, Pixel. Pixel can run very fast, but he can't read maps! First they walk through the Forest of Words. It is cold and windy, so Ben wears his warm jacket.

⛰️ The mountain

Ben's sister is a doctor. She always says: "Never climb in bad weather!" So they wait. At two o'clock the sun comes out, and they climb to the top.

💎 The treasure

At the top there is a wooden box. Inside, there is no gold — there is a mirror! Ben laughs. The real treasure is the explorer in the mirror. Can YOU find the treasure too?

🔎 Did you understand? Tap your answer

💬 Your turn — write your answer

1. Why does Ben laugh at the end? What is the real treasure?
2. You go on a quest! What is in YOUR bag? Write 3 things.
⏭ SKIP-IF-SHORT: the two written questions above can be homework instead.

Teacher note — Step 4 + answer key

Quiz answers: at six o'clock · Pixel · a doctor · a mirror. Open 1 (sample): "The real treasure is Ben himself / the explorer — he is the treasure." Open 2: check there is/are + singular/plural. Annotation hunt: the text hides grammar from every land — is/are, adjectives, -s verbs, there is, wears, weather words, always/never, a doctor (job), can/can't.
🔧 The Grammar Gauntlet — All 9 Lands 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, student says the answer out loud BEFORE you click it — and names the land it comes from!

✅ The BE family

I am ten. She is funny. They are happy.
There is a desk. There are two windows.
I am wearing a jacket. It is sunny.

🔑 Frequency words come AFTER be: She is never late.

🔧 The DO family

He gets up at six. She has a dog.
I love playing football.
I can swim — no -s, no "to"!

🔑 Frequency words come BEFORE these verbs: He always travels.
💡 Tip: before you answer, ask yourself: "Is this a BE sentence or a DO sentence?" That one question unlocks almost every door in Level 1! 🗝️

✏️ The Gauntlet — 10 doors, one from each land (and one extra!)
