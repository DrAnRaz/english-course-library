🔧 Grammar — He Is A… / She Works… 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, student says the answer out loud BEFORE you click it.

✅ JOB = is + A

One person, one job → is a + job.

My mum is a vet. 🐶
He is a cook.
They are farmers. (two people → are + -s, no "a")

🔑 Never forget the little a!

🔧 ACTION = verb + S

He / She does something → the verb gets -s.

She works at a hospital.
He drives a bus.
She has a police car. (have → has!)

🔑 he / she / it → verb + s
💡 The magic pattern: job first, action second.
"My uncle is a farmer. He gets up at five and he has two hundred sheep."

💡 Hungarian tip: Hungarian says "Anya állatorvos" — two words, no "is", no "a". English needs BOTH little words: "My mum is a vet." Count the words: four, not two!
