🏙️ Welcome to Millbrook!

Welcome to Millbrook, a small and friendly town in England! Let me show you around.

🌳 The centre

In the centre, there is a big park. The park has a fountain in the middle and lots of green trees. Next to the park, there is a lovely café with tables outside.

On Saturdays, there is a little market in the park, too. You can buy fruit, flowers and honey there, and there is always happy music.

🏛️ Around the park

Opposite the park, there is the town hall. It is a big white building with tall columns. Between the town hall and the café, there is a car park — very useful for visitors!

🛒 Shops and buses

Near the car park, there is a supermarket. It is open every day from eight o'clock in the morning. In front of the supermarket, there is a bus stop, where people wait for the bus to the city.

Behind the supermarket, there is a quiet river with a small bridge. There are ducks on the river — children love to watch them from the bridge!

🏫 School and library

The school is behind the park. It is a red brick building with a big playground and two football pitches. Next to the school, there is a small library with more than ten thousand books!

In the library, there is a special reading corner with big soft chairs. There isn't a computer room in the library, but there are lots of comics and maps.

🎬 And more…

There is also a cinema, a hospital and three restaurants. There isn't a shopping mall — Millbrook is a small, quiet town. But there are lots of friendly people, and it is a wonderful place to live!

The cinema is small — there is only one screen — but the films are great, and the popcorn is famous in the whole town.

Would you like to live in Millbrook? What is there in YOUR town?

🔧 Grammar — there is / there are + Where Is It? 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, student says the answer BEFORE you click it.

✅ ONE thing — there is

There is a park. 🌳
There isn't a cinema.
Is there a hospital? — Yes, there is.

🔑 + preposition = a real description:
There is a library next to the school.

👥 MANY things — there are

There are two cafés. ☕☕
There aren't any buses.
Are there any shops? — No, there aren't.

🔑 negative & question with many → add any
🧭 Directions — the traveller's four magic lines: "Excuse me, where is the library?" → "Go straight on. Turn left at the square. Turn right at the café. It's on your left!"

💡 Hungarian tip: Hungarian starts with the place — "A parkban VAN egy szökőkút." English starts with There: "There is a fountain in the park" — NOT "In the park is a fountain". Always launch the sentence with "There is / There are".
