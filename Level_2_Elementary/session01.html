⚡ Warm-Up — Yum or Yuck? 5 minStep 2
📹 Zoom: pure talking game — student answers with the 👍 or 👎 reaction FIRST, then says the sentence.

How to play:

1. Your teacher says a food word: "Pizza!"
2. You react fast: 👍 Yum! or 👎 Yuck!
3. Then say the full sentence: "I like pizza!" or "I don't like pizza!"
4. After 5 foods, switch — YOU say the foods, your teacher reacts.

No score here — just wake up your English brain! 🧠

Teacher note — Step 2

🍕 Food around the world — by Priya, 12

Hi! My name is Priya and I am twelve years old. I love food — let me tell you!

🍝 My favourite food

My favourite food is pasta. I eat pasta three times a week! I really like tomato pasta and cheese pasta.

I also love pizza. My perfect pizza has mushrooms and olives on top.

On Fridays, my dad and I make pizza at home. I put the cheese on top, and he puts the pizza in the oven. I really like cooking with my dad!

🌶️ Foods I don't like

But I don't like everything. I don't like spicy food at all. When I eat something spicy, my eyes water and my face goes red!

I don't like fish very much either. My mum loves fish, but I think it smells funny.

🧀 My friend Marco

My friend Marco likes very different food. He likes rice and chicken, and he loves plantain — a type of banana that you cook.

Marco doesn't like cheese. He says it is too strong. We always laugh, because I love cheese and he hates it!

At school, we sometimes share our lunch. Marco gives me some rice and chicken, and I give him some tomato pasta. He likes my mum's pasta a lot!

🥤 What I drink

With lunch, I usually drink water or apple juice. I don't like milk very much, but I love hot chocolate in winter.

My little brother is very different. He loves milk! He drinks it with breakfast, with lunch and with dinner. Mum says he is half cat!

🌍 One world, many tastes

In Japan, people eat sushi — raw fish with rice. In Mexico, people eat delicious tacos. In India, people love spicy curry.

One day, I want to visit all of these countries and try the food. Do you like trying new food? I do — well, when it is not spicy!

Everyone likes different things, and that is fantastic. What do you love? What don't you like?

🔎 Did you understand? Tap your answer

🔧 Grammar — like / don't like / Do you like…? 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, student says the answer BEFORE you click it.

✅ SAYING IT — like / don't like

I like pizza. 🍕
I don't like fish.
She likes pasta. ← the magic -s!
He doesn't like eggs. ← -s hides in does

🔑 like + food, or like + verb-ing: I like cooking.

❓ ASKING IT — Do you like…?

Do you like chocolate?
→ Yes, I do. / No, I don't.

Does she like pasta?
→ Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.

🔑 he / she / it → does
💡 The taste scale: 😍 I love → 😊 I like → 😐 It's OK → 😕 I don't like → 🤢 I hate. Point at the scale and place today's foods on it!
💪 How much? really / quite / absolutely + like/love: I absolutely love chocolate! (number one! 😍) · I really like pizza. (a lot 👍) · I quite like salad. (it's OK 😐)

💡 Hungarian tip: in Hungarian you say "Szeretem A pizzát" — with "a". In English, general likes drop "the": "I like pizza", NOT "I like the pizza". And remember: she likes, but she doesn't like (the -s moves into does).
