⚡ Warm-Up — Champion Battle 5 minStep 2
📹 Zoom: pure talking game, cameras on. Use the 👍 reaction when the sentence is a good one. No screen needed yet.

How to play:

1. Your teacher says two things — for example: "a cheetah… and a snail!" 🐆🐌
2. You say ONE sentence to compare them: "The cheetah is faster than the snail."
3. Then swap: YOU pick two things, the teacher compares them. Try to make the teacher laugh! 😄

No score here — just wake up your English brain! 🧠

Teacher note — Step 2

Good pairs: elephant/mouse, pizza/broccoli, summer/winter, phone/TV, your town/London. Accept ANY comparison attempt. If the student is stuck, model: "The elephant is bigger. The mouse is smaller." Keep it silly and fast.

🏆 Our trip to the Record Zone — by Kofi, 11

Last Friday my class visited the Record Zone — a museum about world records. It was the best school trip of the year!

🐆 Animal champions

First we saw the animal hall. The cheetah is the fastest land animal. It can run 120 kilometres per hour!

The blue whale is bigger than thirty elephants. It is the biggest animal in the world.

🧍 People records

Then we saw a photo of the tallest man in history. He was 2 metres and 72 centimetres tall! My friend Priya is the tallest student in our class, but next to him she looked tiny.

🦖 The funny hall

The funniest record was a man who ran a marathon in a dinosaur costume. It was hotter than a normal race, and much slower. My teacher laughed so hard that she dropped her sandwich.

🚌 The bus game

On the bus home, we played a game: find the strangest record. Priya won again. She found a snail race in England. The winning snail was slower than my grandad on the stairs — and grandad is really slow!

Which record would you break? Maybe you are faster, funnier or braver than you think. 💪

Teacher note — Step 4 + answer key

Quiz answers: 120 km per hour · thirty elephants · 2 m 72 cm · a marathon in a dinosaur costume. Open 1: because the man in the photo was much taller than her (2.72 m). 🔧 Grammar — Comparatives & Superlatives 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, the student says the answer out loud BEFORE you click it.

⚖️ COMPARATIVE — comparing TWO

short word + -er … THAN
A cheetah is faster than a horse. 🐆

long word → MORE … than
A shark is more dangerous than a goldfish. 🦈

🔑 Spelling: big → bigger · funny → funnier

👑 SUPERLATIVE — the champion of ALL

THE + short word + -est
The blue whale is the biggest animal. 🐋

THE MOST + long word
The most expensive pizza costs $2000! 💎

🔑 Don't forget THE!
💡 The rebel words — they follow NO rules:
good → better → the best 👍 · bad → worse → the worst 👎

💡 Hungarian tip: Hungarian adds "-bb" to every adjective (nagyobb, legnagyobb). English splits the job: SHORT words get -er/-est, LONG words get more/most. And "than" = "mint" — don't skip it!
