⚡ Warm-Up — Yum or Yuck? 5 minStep 2
📹 Zoom: cameras on, no screen yet. Use 👍 = yum, 👎 = yuck reactions too.

How to play:

1. Teacher says a food. "Pizza!" 🍕
2. You say "Yum!" 😋 or "Yuck!" 😝
3. Show it with your face too!
4. Now YOU say foods. Teacher answers!

No score here. Funny faces are good! 🧠

Teacher note — Step 2

Start with foods the student surely knows (pizza, banana, chocolate). Mix in one silly item ("Spider soup!") for a guaranteed laugh and a loud "YUCK!". Goal: the yum/yuck reflex is automatic before Step 3.

Teacher note — Step 4 + answer key

Quiz answers: yellow · pizza · carrots · cold. Open questions — any food is fine; single words OK ("pizza", "soup"). Echo back the full chunk: "I like pizza. Yum!" Keep the yum/yuck gestures going on every line — the body remembers what the ear forgets.
🔧 Magic Pattern — "I like ___" 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. Student SAYS each answer out loud BEFORE you click it.

😋 I LIKE — yum!

Point at food. Smile. Say:

I like apples.
I like pizza.
I like cake.

🔑 Then say: Yum! 😋

😝 I DON'T LIKE — yuck!

Point at food. Funny face. Say:

I don't like soup.
I don't like carrots.

🔑 Then say: Yuck! 😝
💡 The pattern: I like ___. Yum! / I don't like ___. Yuck! Same music every time! 🎵

💡 Hungarian tip: English says "I like appleS" — many apples! Hungarian says "Szeretem az almát" — just one. Add the S sound! 🍎🍎🍎

✏️ Practice — tap the correct one

Teacher note — Step 5

Pattern only — never explain "don't" as an auxiliary. Teach it as one sound-chunk: "I-don't-like". Chant the two patterns with gestures before the quiz. 6+/8 → play a speed round with the vocab cards; under 5 → back to choral chant with 🔊.
