🔧 Patterns — Name Talk 10 minStep 5
📹 Zoom: share screen. For each quiz item, student SAYS the answer before you click it.

💬 THE NAME QUESTION

One question. One answer. Always the same!

"What is your name?" 🤔
"My name is Tam." 😊

🔑 Pattern: My name is + name.

🔤 SPELL IT!

Say your name letter by letter.

"How do you spell it?"
"T-A-M. Tam!" ✏️

🔑 Pattern: letter, letter, letter — name!
💡 Magic mini-talk — say it 3 times, faster each time:
"What is your name?" — "My name is Isa. I-S-A!"

💡 Hungarian tip: English letter names are DIFFERENT: A = "éj", E = "í", I = "áj". Trust the 🔊 sound, not the Hungarian letter!

✏️ Practice — tap the right answer

Teacher note — Step 5

Chunks only, no grammar talk. The A/E/I letter-name confusion is THE classic Hungarian trap — drill those three with the 🔊 buttons until automatic. Then spell the student's real name together, letter by letter, twice.

🤸 MOVE! — 1 minute break

Stand up! Air-write your name in GIANT letters. ✍️ Use your whole arm! Teacher guesses each letter. Then air-write a big X and a big O!
